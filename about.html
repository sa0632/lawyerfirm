Our Story

Founded on
Integrity & Justice

Smith Legal Group was founded in 1998 by James R. Smith with a singular vision: to provide every client with the same quality of legal representation regardless of their background or resources. What started as a small two-attorney practice in Manhattan has grown into one of the nation's most respected full-service law firms.

Today, with over 120 attorneys across 6 practice areas and offices in New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, and Miami, we continue to uphold the founding principle that justice is not a privilege — it is a right.
